The green-cheeked conure is typically 26 cm (10 in) long and weighs 60 to 80 g.

It is mainly green, with a brown/black/grey crown, white periophthalmic rings, green cheeks, blue primary wing feathers, a grey beak, and its long pointed tail is mostly maroon.

It has short transverse striations on its breast and a red abdominal area. Males and females have an identical external appearance.
